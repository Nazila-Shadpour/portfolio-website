Movie Mock-Up

Project Overview

This movie mock-up project was a collaborative effort completed by a team of four. Each team member created their own movie design using Photoshop in a previous class. After reviewing designs, we decided to move forward with one team member’s concept. Based on that, we developed a detailed prototype using Adobe XD to visualize the overall layout, design elements, and user interactions. The prototype shows how the app should look and function, and it helped guide the development of our next project.

Project Details

The Process

At the start of the project, we divided the work into smaller tasks, assigning specific pages to each team member. This approach allowed us to focus on different parts of the prototype efficiently. We regularly shared our ideas and kept each other updated on design decisions to ensure consistency across all platforms—mobile, tablet, and desktop. Clear communication and regular check-ins helped us stay aligned and complete the project smoothly within the timeline.

Reflection

This project was a valuable opportunity to work in a team and strengthen my collaboration skills, mockup design, and prototype development. One minor challenge was ensuring consistency across pages as we each worked on different parts of the design.

I learned how important the design and planning phase is for creating a clear and effective layout before starting development.

In the future, the design could be improved by adding casting information, YouTube trailer links, and a movie search feature to enhance the user experience.
